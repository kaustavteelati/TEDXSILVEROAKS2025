About TED

TED is a nonprofit, nonpartisan organization dedicated to discovering, debating and spreading ideas that spark conversation, deepen understanding and drive meaningful change.

Our organization is devoted to curiosity, reason, wonder and the pursuit of knowledge — without an agenda. We welcome people from every discipline and culture who seek a deeper understanding of the world and connection with others, and we invite everyone to engage with ideas and activate them in your community.

About TEDx

X = Independently Organized

In the spirit of discovering and spreading ideas, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience.

At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event.

The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized.

Maya: Illusions in Daily Life

Maya: The Illusions in Daily Life

The ancient Sanskrit word "Maya" often translates to "illusion," but it signifies more than mere trickery. It represents the powerful, enchanting force that constructs our perceived reality—the veil that separates us from a fundamental truth.

In this edition of TEDxSilverOaksIntSchoolBachupally, we deconstruct the 'Maya' of the 21st century:

  • The Illusion of Self: Are our personalities fixed, or fluid constructs?
  • The Illusion of Time: Why do we feel so time-poor in an age of efficiency?
  • The Illusion of Connection: Is social media bringing us together or building walls?
  • The Illusion of Certainty: In a complex world, why do we crave simple answers?

TEDxSilver Oaks Intl School Bachupally invites speakers to question and reveal what lies beyond appearances, and to help others see through the veil of illusion.
