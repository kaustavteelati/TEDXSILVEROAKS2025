Our History

Discover the journey of TEDxSilveroaks through the years

2023

The Beginning

TEDxSilveroaks was born from a vision to create a platform for sharing innovative ideas within our school community. A dedicated team of students and faculty came together to organize our first independent TEDx event.

2024

First Official Event

Our inaugural TEDxSilveroaks event took place with the theme "Breaking Barriers". We featured 8 inspiring speakers from various fields who shared their perspectives on overcoming limitations and pushing boundaries.

2025

Expanding Horizons

This year, we explore "Maya: Illusions in Daily Life", delving into the concept of perception versus reality in our modern world. Our event has grown to include workshops, interactive sessions, and a diverse lineup of thought leaders.

Future

Continuing the Legacy

We aim to continue fostering a culture of curiosity and innovation, bringing together bright minds to share ideas that inspire positive change in our community and beyond.

About TEDxSilveroaks

Learn more about our mission and impact

Our Purpose

TEDxSilveroaks International School Bachupally is a student-led initiative that brings the spirit of TED's mission to discover "ideas worth spreading" to our local community.

We believe in the power of ideas to change attitudes, lives, and ultimately, the world. Our independently organized TEDx events aim to create a platform where thinkers and doers can share insights that spark deep discussion and connection.

Through our events, we strive to inspire curiosity, foster innovation, and build a community of lifelong learners who are passionate about making a positive impact.
